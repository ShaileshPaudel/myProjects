Clark Dining Hall


Clark is a popular dining hall at NC State University, located by the Frank Thompson Performance Hall, and can be accessed by meal swipes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Although it usually closes an hour earlier than the more popular Fountain, Clark offers a smaller, cozy alternative where many students love to grab a meal.

Check out the full website to view more information, including information about location and hours!
